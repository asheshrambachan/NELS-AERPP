File:       BYF4STU
Name:       F4UNIV1
Position:   8
Length:     4
Label:      Sample member status in all five waves

Description:
Sample member status in all five waves
     Indicates simultaneously the base year, first follow-up, second
follow-up, third follow-up, and fourth follow-up situations of
sample members.  This variable has valid values that account for
every pattern encountered by NELS:88 fourth follow-up respondents.
Note, however, that there are gaps in the range of codes displayed,
since not all codes apply to the respondents. Value labels begin
with BY status, followed by F1 and other follow-up statuses.
Responses are sorted in order of the common participation patterns.
              VARIABLE LABEL DEFINITIONS
BY=Base Year                         F1=First Follow-up
F2=Second Follow-up                  F3=Third Follow-up
F4=Fourth Follow-up                  BNA=Base Year Not Applicable
1NA=First Follow-up Not Applicable   A=In-school, in grade
B=In-School out-of-grade             D=Dropout
E=Eligible                           F=Freshened
G=Received GED/HS Equivalent         H=Received HS diploma
I=Ineligible                         N=Not Pursuing GED/HS Diploma
O=Subsampled Out or Equivalent       P=Pursuing GED/HS Diploma
X=Out of Scope (e.g., deceased)      1ER=F1 Sampling Error
2ER=F2 Sampling Error                ?=Status Unknown
Q=Respondents
For example, cases where F4UNIV1 = 1213 relate to sample members with
the following participation patterns:  "BNA  1NA  F2FA  F3H  F4Q."
In other words, the sample members were not study applicable during
the BY and F1 waves, were freshened into the sample during F2 (when
they were in school and on grade [the 12th]), had received a high
school diploma by F3, and were respondents during F4.
Applies to:  All respondents
Sources: NELS:88/2000

Name:       G8MINOR
Position:   385
Length:     3
Label:      PERCENT MINORITY IN SCHOOL

Description:
G8MINOR reflects the percentage of minority students in the
eighth grade reported by the school. It was constructed by
adding nonreserve code values of BYSC13A-D and categorizing
the result.  If the school questionnaire was missing or if
BYSC13A-D was missing, G8MINOR was set to missing.
000 = None                006 = 61-90%
001 = 1-5%                007 = 91-100%
002 = 6-10%               998 = Missing
003 = 11-20%
004 = 21-40%
005 = 41-60%
NOTE:  This variable was previously delivered in a single-digit
format.  It is presented here in a three-digit format.
This variable was recoded on the public data file
by NCES in accordance with the confidentiality
provisions of PL100-297 (1988).
/*-----------------------------------------------------------------*/
/*   Create composite G8MINOR by adding all non-reserve code values
for variables BYSC13A-D together. */
G8MINOR = 0;  /* Initialize by setting to zero */
ARRAY RECODE BYSC13A--BYSC13D;  /* Process all variables the same */
DO OVER RECODE;
IF RECODE GE 996 THEN RECODE=.; /* If GE 996, set to missing */
END;
/*   If all variables are missing, set to 998 (mis). Otherwise, set
equal to the sum of BYSC13A-D.  */
IF BYSC13A = . AND BYSC13B = . AND BYSC13C = . AND BYSC13D = .
THEN G8MINOR = 998;
ELSE G8MINOR = SUM(BYSC13A,BYSC13B,BYSC13C,BYSC13D);
/* Finally, if sum of variables is missing, set G8MINOR to missing.*/
IF G8MINOR = . THEN G8MINOR = 998;
Sources: NELS:88/94 public-use ECB

Name:       G8LUNCH
Position:   388
Length:     3
Label:      PERCENT FREE LUNCH IN SCHOOL

Description:
G8LUNCH categorizes the percentage of free or reduced price
lunch at the school calculated from the school
questionnaire. It was constructed by dividing BYSC16A by
BYSC2, multiplying by 100, rounding to the nearest whole
number and coding the result. If the school questionnaire
was missing or if BYSC16A was missing, G8LUNCH was set to
missing.  The value for G8LUNCH are:
000 = None                005 = 31-50%
001 = 1-5%                006 = 51-75%
002 = 6-10%               007 = 76-100%
003 = 11-20%              998 = Missing
004 = 21-30%
NOTE:  This variable was previously delivered in a single-digit
format.  It is presented here in a three-digit format.
This variable was recoded on the public data file
by NCES in accordance with the confidentiality
provisions of PL100-297 (1988).
/*-----------------------------------------------------------------*/
/*   Create composite G8LUNCH.  If BYSC2 is not missing and not a
value between 9996 and 9999, and BYSC16A is not missing and not
a value between 9996 and 9999, then create G8LUNCH by rounding
off the quotient of 100*BYSC16A divided by BYSC2.  Otherwise,

Description:
G10CTRL1 classifies the type of school into public,
Catholic, other religious and nonsectarian private schools,
as reported by the school.  The classification was
constructed from F1C4 and F1C4A.  See the F1 Student
Component Data File User's Manual for explication of the
logic behind the construction of G10CTRL1.
Earlier releases of this variable coded all dropouts as
Not Enrolled in School.  In the current version of this
variable, all schools named by the sample members are
included in this variable, regardless of whether the school
named was a NELS:88 sampled school or not.  Dropouts who
did not complete a questionnaire or who did not indicate
their last school attended were coded as Missing (98).
NOTE:  This variable includes data for dropouts and
non-respondents.

by NCES in accordance with the confidentiality provisions
of PL100-297 (1988).
/*-----------------------------------------------------------------*/
/*   Create composite G10CTRL1 using F1C4 and F1C4AA-M from school
file.
Set G10CTRL1 equal to 1 (Public) if:
a) F1C4 equals 1 and any of F1C4AA-E or K-M equals 1 or 8, or
b) F1C4 equals 8 and any of F1C4AA-C equals 1 and none of
F1C4AF-J are equal to 1.
Set G10CTRL1 equal to 2 (Catholic) if F1C4 equals 2 or 8,
any of F1C4F-H equal 1 and neither F1C4J or I equals 1.
Set G10CTRL1 equal to 3 (Private-Other religious affiliation) if
F1C4 equals either 2 or 8, F1C4AI equals 1 and none of F1C4AF-H
or J equals 1.
Set G10CTRL1 equal to 4 (Private-No religious affiliation) if
F1C4 equals either 2 or 8, F1C4AJ equals 1 and none of F1C4AF-I
equals 1.
Set G10CTRL1 equal to 5 (Private-affiliation unknown) if F1C4
equals 2 and none of F1C4AA-C or F-J equal 1.
Otherwise, set G10CTRL1 to missing. */
IF (F1C4 = 1 & (F1C4AA = 1 OR F1C4AB = 1 OR F1C4AC = 1 OR
F1C4AD = 1 OR F1C4AE = 1 OR F1C4AK = 1 OR F1C4AL = 1 OR
F1C4AM = 1 OR F1C4AA = 8 OR F1C4AB = 8 OR F1C4AC = 8 OR
F1C4AD = 8 OR F1C4AE = 8 OR F1C4AK = 8 OR F1C4AL = 8 OR
F1C4AM = 8 )) OR
(F1C4 = 8 & ((F1C4AA = 1 OR F1C4AB = 1 OR F1C4AC = 1) &
(F1C4AF NE 1 & F1C4AG NE 1 & F1C4AH NE 1 & F1C4AI NE 1 &
F1C4AJ NE 1 ))) THEN G10CTRL1 = 1;                   /* Public */
ELSE IF (F1C4 = 2 OR F1C4 = 8) & (F1C4AF = 1 OR F1C4AG = 1 OR
F1C4AH = 1) & (F1C4AJ NE 1 & F1C4AI NE 1)
THEN G10CTRL1 = 2;                                   /* Catholic */
ELSE IF (F1C4 = 2 OR F1C4 = 8) & (F1C4AI = 1) & (F1C4AF NE 1 &
F1C4AG NE 1 & F1C4AH NE 1 & F1C4AJ NE 1)
THEN G10CTRL1 = 3;             /* Private - Other religious aff. */
ELSE IF (F1C4 = 2 OR F1C4 = 8) & (F1C4AJ = 1) & (F1C4AF NE 1 &
F1C4AG NE 1 & F1C4AH NE 1 & F1C4AI NE 1)
THEN G10CTRL1 = 4;                /* Private - No religious aff. */
ELSE IF (F1C4 = 2) & (F1C4AA NE 1 & F1C4AB NE 1 & F1C4AC NE 1 &
F1C4AF NE 1 & F1C4AG NE 1 & F1C4AH NE 1 & F1C4AI NE 1 &
F1C4AJ NE 1 )
THEN G10CTRL1 = 5;                      /* Private - Aff. unkown */
ELSE G10CTRL1 = 98;
